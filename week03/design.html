WDD 131 Design Principles

Alignment

Alignment creates a visual connection between related elements, making content easier to scan and understand.

Contrast

Contrast helps important elements stand out and improves readability, especially for accessibility.

Repetition

Repetition strengthens design by tying together otherwise separate parts and creating consistency.
